Doktora (2010-2016)

DOKUZ EYLÜL ÜNİVERSİTESİ,FEN BİLİMLERİ ENSTİTÜSÜ, BİLGİSAYAR MÜHENDİSLİĞİ BÖLÜMÜ

Tez Başlığı: Energy Efficient Wireless Sensor Networks For Underground Mining Applications

Tez Konusu: In This Study, A Low-cost, Low-power Wireless Sensor Network (WSN) Architecture Is Proposed To Monitor Active And Abandoned Underground Mining Workplaces. The Proposed Wsn Will Monitor The Presence Of Temperature, Humidity, Methane And Carbon Monoxide Parameters From Underground Mining Workplaces In Order To Avoid Mine Accidents. For This Purpose, Arduino Suitable Sensor Nodes Were Developed For Underground Coal Mine Monitoring Application. The Developed Sensor Nodes Were Tested In Terms Of Radio Signal Propagation Through Different Materials. Additionally, The Coverage Of Sensor Nodes Was Measured In Closed And Open Field Experiments. Finally, The Signal Range Of The Sensor Nodes Were Measured Under The Soil.

Tarih: 30 Eylül 2016

Tez Danışmanı: YALÇIN ÇEBİ (Kayıtlı Kullanıcı)

Eğitim: Mühendislik ve Teknolojik Bilimler

Eğitim Alt Alanı: BILGISAYAR MUHENDISLIGI BOLUMU

Yüksek Lisans(2006-2010) Tezli Program

DOKUZ EYLÜL ÜNİVERSİTESİ FEN BİLİMLERİ ENSTİTÜSÜ, BİLGİSAYAR MÜHENDİSLİĞİ (YL) (TEZLİ)

Tez Başlığı: Obtaining The Three-dimensional Structure Of The Current Environment By Using Fiber Grating Laser System

Tez Konusu: It Is A Necessity That The Mobile Robots Should Determine First The Obstacles In The Environment In Which They Are Located, And Then Depending On Their Dimensions, The Free Areas Between The Obstacles. In Order To Find The Position Of The Mobile Robot And The Locations Of The Obstacles Around It, Systems Which Sense The Environment With The Help Of The Camera And Provide Facilities For Self Guidance Of The Mobile Robot, Were Developed Based On The Development Of Information Technologies.ın The Scope Of This Project, An Omnidirectional Vision System, Which Can Be Used For Mobile Robots And Was Intended To Determine Both The Obstacles In The Environment And The Places Through Which A Mobile Robot Can Pass, By Sensing The Environment In A Three Dimensional Manner, Was Developed.

Tarih: 30 Temmuz 2010

Tez Danışmanı: YALÇIN ÇEBİ (Kayıtlı Kullanıcı)
